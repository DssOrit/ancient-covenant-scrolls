Verse to Video

How it works

Turn any verse, lyric, or short passage into editable scene cards

Paste a verse, pick a global style + a character bible if you have one, tap Build. The tool breaks the passage into scene cards with visual prompts, suggested camera + lighting, and clip duration. Export the scene-cards JSON and feed it to the Book-to-Video engine in Load main, or hand the per-scene prompts to any AI image / video provider.
